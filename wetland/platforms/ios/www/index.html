    About this app

    Nebraska Wetland mobile application is a product to engage citizen participation in wetland Nebraska conservation. The overall goal is to develop an interative mobile platform that transfers the Nebraska wetland information to mobile devices and also allow stakeholders and citizens to report the real-time observation information to wetland managers. The major functions of this app include:

    (1) View Nebraska wetland program information and educational materials;

    (2) View the National Wetland Inventory(NWI) map on mobile devices;

    (3) View the Soil Survey Geographic(SSURGO) database on mobile devices;

    (4) Report information (message, photo, video) for Wetland Monitoring and Assessment; Wetland Management and Restoration; Wetland Permitting and Mitigation

    (5) Link with social media and network for wetland conservation;
